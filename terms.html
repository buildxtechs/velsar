01

Payment Terms

An advance payment is required to confirm the order. Production will begin only after receipt of the advance amount.

80% Advance Payment Required

Remaining 20% balance must be fully cleared before dispatch of the bulk garments.

Approved payment methods accepted:

  • Direct Bank Wire Transfer (RTGS / NEFT / IMPS)
  • Unified Payments Interface (UPI Transfers)
  • Other mutually approved corporate banking options
02

Price Guidelines

We ensure highly competitive corporate pricing structures under the following parameters:

  • All prices are exclusive of Goods and Services Tax (GST), shipping fees, and other applicable port/custom charges unless otherwise explicitly mentioned.
  • Quoted prices are strictly valid for **15 days** from the date of quotation issuance.
  • Final prices may fluctuate based on current yarn/fabric costs, structural printing processes, washing styles, accessories, and individual customization complexity.
03

Production & Delivery

Our production department operates systematically to honor deliveries, governed by the following timelines:

  • Production schedules are closely dependent on order size, fabric mill availability, printing techniques, and intermediate client approvals.
  • Delivery lead times begin **only after** receiving the required 80% advance payment and receiving signed/written approval of final pre-production samples.
  • All shipping and freight charges are billed extra unless specified otherwise in the proforma invoice.

Force Majeure Timeline Extensions

Delays caused by third-party transport companies, extreme weather conditions, or unforeseen machinery breakdowns are not under direct company responsibility.

04

Sample Approval

To avoid fitment and printing errors, we follow strict sample confirmation checks:

Bulk production will commence **only** after the client confirms official approval on:

  • Fit samples, size specs, and pattern layouts
  • Graphic artwork, dimensions, and positioning sheets
  • Wash style, colors, and raw textile qualities

Modifications After Approval

Any adjustments requested post-sample approval will increase bulk production costs, extend delivery timelines, and incur extra sampling fees.

05

Quality Tolerance Guidelines

Products are manufactured in strict accordance with approved samples. However, due to the nature of bulk industrial textile processing, standard industry tolerances apply:

Specification Metric Acceptable Tolerance Range
Size Measurements (Width / Length) ±0.5 to 1.0 inch variation
Fabric Weight Density (GSM) Minor variations (±5%) within mill production limits
Color Shading Variance Slight variation between different dyeing bath batches

These minor tolerances are universally considered acceptable within wholesale export production standards.

06

Branding, Labels & Printing

All customization and accessory details must be finalized prior to bulk manufacturing initiation. This includes:

  • Neck Labels (Woven or Heat-Transfer)
  • Wash Care Labels and country origin tags
  • Hang Tags, barcode tickets, and custom poly-packaging
  • Embroidery artwork and screen-printing films

Artworks and Spelling Errors Disclaimer

Velsar Apparels is not responsible for printing/spelling errors contained in final artwork approved and confirmed by the client.

07

Special Process & Wash Disclaimer

Clients ordering specialty washes, pigment treatments, and premium graphic printing should note that:

Processes including **Puff Printing, HD Printing, DTF/DTG prints, Acid washes, Lava dyeing, Tie-Dye, Spray/Distressing effects, and Stone/Sand blasting** may display slight differences in:

  • Overall vintage washing finish and base color shades
  • Print placement coordinates and raise texture dimensions
  • Tactile print thickness due to the manual/chemical nature of printing techniques.

These slight variances represent unique artisan finishes and are not classified as manufacturing defects.

08

Damages & Shortages

In massive industrial bulk apparel sewing and processing runs, minor defects or count variations are recognized as standard production factors.

Up to 10% Tolerable Minor Defect Margin

Variations within 10% combined margin in high-volume runs are acceptable under wholesale standards.

All claims must be submitted to Velsar Apparels within **48 hours** of package receipt. Submissions must include:

  • High-resolution photos showing the issue
  • Continuous unboxing or inspection videos
  • Copy of the corresponding invoice

⚠️ No claims are accepted for garments that have been washed, alterated, retail-tagged, or worn by customers.

09

Returns & Refunds

Since all garments are custom-made to client color, measurements, and design specifications, the following return policy applies:

  • Returns are strictly not accepted once bulk fabrics have been dyed/cut.
  • Order cancellations are not permitted after production has officially commenced.
  • Refunds or replacement runs are only applicable to major structural manufacturing defects (e.g. raw fabric holes, completely incorrect sizing) verified by our QC checkers.

Approved refunds are processed to the source bank account within **7 to 14 working days**.

10

Order Changes or Cancellation

Any request to change quantities, size sets, or artwork after initial order confirmation may:

  • Increase final production unit costs
  • Delay confirmed shipment schedules
  • Require extra fabric/accessory procurement costs.

Orders canceled by the client after manufacturing commencement will result in **complete forfeiture of the 80% advance payment** to recover fabric and raw resource costs.

11

Packaging & Dispatch

We provide high-quality export-grade packaging as standard:

  • Garments are individually folded, packed in secure polybags, and structured in heavy-duty master cartons.
  • Special packing requests (e.g. hanger-packing, bio-degradable bags, barcode stickers on bags) must be requested before proforma invoicing and may incur additional charges.
  • Dispatch and logistics tracking numbers are shared immediately upon cargo loading.
12

Delays Due to Client

Any delay in providing the following checkpoints from the client side will directly extend the guaranteed delivery timeline:

  • Staging advance payment confirmations
  • Approving print layouts, sizing sheets, or custom labels
  • Confirming wash styles or custom dye laboratory lap-dips

In such instances, Velsar Apparels reserves the right to issue a revised production and delivery schedule.

13

Intellectual Property Rights

We respect design ownership and legal copyright guidelines:

  • All logo files, graphic prints, typography, and clothing brand designs submitted by clients are assumed to have official authorization and ownership rights.
  • Velsar Apparels acts solely as a contract manufacturer and is not liable for copyright, trademark, or legal disputes related to submitted branding materials.
14

Confidentiality Agreement

Velsar Apparels is committed to protecting your creative assets. All technical custom specifications, private pattern cuts, logo artworks, and brand marketing concepts shared with us are treated with strict confidentiality. These details will never be shared with third parties or used for any purposes other than contract bulk manufacturing.

15

Force Majeure

Velsar Apparels is not liable for manufacturing delays or delivery failures caused by events outside of our reasonable control. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Natural disasters (floods, earthquakes, storms)
  • Government restrictions, lockdowns, or custom/export border holds
  • Labor strikes, union disputes, or regional electricity/power grid gridlocks
  • Pandemics, regional lockdowns, or nationwide logistics closures.
16

Wholesale Manufacturing Policy

As contract bulk garment manufacturers:

  • Minor variations in stitching density, print alignments, wash shades, or tolerances are considered standard industry deviations.
  • Custom washing and distressing processes (e.g. vintage washes) are executed by hand; slight differences from reference samples are typical.
17

Inspection Before Dispatch

We maintain strict AQL 2.5 checkpoints before any cargo leaves our Tiruppur factory floor. Clients or third-party agencies are welcome to coordinate inspections. We advise clients to verify overall quantity and package condition immediately upon arrival.

18

Communication & Approvals

To ensure absolute clarity, approvals shared via:

  • Corporate Email channels
  • WhatsApp Business chat sessions
  • Signed digital/written mockups

will be treated as official, binding approvals for final pattern cuts and bulk screen-printing prints.

19

Legal Jurisdiction

All business transactions, proforma invoices, and bulk contracts are governed by local trade policies. Any disputes arising between the client and Velsar Apparels are subject strictly and exclusively to the legal jurisdiction of **Tiruppur Courts, Tamil Nadu, India** only.

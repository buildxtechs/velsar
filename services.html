Industrial Production Standards

Factory Work Process & Guidelines

We adhere strictly to international manufacturing workflows, ensuring every phase—from raw yarn circular knitting to final AQL 2.5 retail dispatch—is certified for excellence.

01

Knitting

Advanced circular knitting looms convert premium combed and organic yarns into dense, high-grade single jerseys, fleeces, and rib structures.

02

Dyeing

Eco-friendly international standard dyeing vessels apply vibrant, organic color pigment setups featuring superior colorfastness scores.

03

Fabric Roll

Dyed fabrics are stabilized, relaxed, and wrapped in secure rolls to allow organic structures to recover elastic qualities prior to processing.

04

GSM Check

Rigorous fabric weight and thickness auditing. Electronic weight scales verify the exact GSM matches our clients fabric specifications.

05

Cutting Machine 1 & 2

CAD-guided precision slicing systems. Computerized heavy duty band knife and straight cutting machines 1 & 2 handle layered panels without pucker.

06

Stitching

High-speed professional Pegasus overlock and flatlock floors secure structural joints, double-collar reinforce tapes, and smooth edge trims.

07

Checking

100% manual panel audit. Master checkers verify stitching integrity, detect needle marks, and enforce absolute symmetry guidelines.

08

Ironing & Packing

Premium vacuum steam ironing eliminates creases. Garments are individually tagged, boxed, and packed in custom protective polybags.

09

Finishing Goods

Final export quality audit (AQL 2.5) check. The clean packed batches are logged into dispatch inventory for direct global retail distribution.

Bespoke Graphics Finishes

Specialty Printing Services

We carry advanced semi-automatic screen-printing carousels and professional industrial printers to achieve vibrant graphics.

DTF (Direct-to-Film)

Exceptional high-definition full-color graphic prints. Ideal for complex artworks, photographic prints, and multi-colored brand logos with seamless gradients.

DTG (Direct-to-Garment)

Uses eco-friendly water-based inks sprayed directly into fabric fibers. Extremely soft feel, zero-feel hand, and excellent wash durability on custom retail styles.

Puff & 3D Ink Printing

Specialty expanding screen print ink that rises when heated, creating raised, pillowy volumetric lettering. Highly sought-after in premium streetwear lines.

Industrial Screen Printing

The industry benchmark for massive bulk orders. Exceptional economy at scale, vibrant plastisol inks, clean water-based prints, or discharge inks for breathable graphics.

Reflective & Neon Prints

Incorporates high-visibility micro-glass beads that shine back when hit by lights. Excellent detail overlays on athletic gear, active wear, and urban streetwear.

Patch Work & Applique

Embroidery integration with fabric layers. Custom twill, denim, or leather lettering sewn down securely to add tactile premium depth to crewnecks.

Silicon Gel & Stone Work

High-end crystal setups, raised hot-fix rhinestone placements, and thick silicon gel overlays that add glossy futuristic plastic grip textures.

Embroidery & Discharge

Intricate multi-head computer threading combined with chemical discharge color-stripping for vintage, ultra-soft luxury embellishments.

Premium Fabric Customization

Premium Washing & Dyeing Effects

We apply highly technical enzyme weathering, vintage dyeings, and protective softeners to achieve unmatched streetwear comfort.

Acid Wash Finish

Achieves a highly sought-after vintage weathered look. Each garment undergoes specific marbled fading, resulting in an exceptionally soft broke-in feel.

Lava Dyeing Wash

An advanced dye wash targeting structural seams and necklines. Delivers beautiful, organic color transitions and vintage high-contrast shading.

CPT (Cold Pigment Wash)

Creates a striking distressed surface texture. The pigment bonds dynamically to outer layers, ensuring distinct washed structural seam highlights.

Optic Brightening Wash

A specialized bio-chemical wash that protects white jersey fibers from yellowing, maintaining stark optic white brightness through wash cycles.

Artisanal Tie & Dye

Hand-bound and pigmented by master dyers in custom swirl patterns. Offers bright contrast options that remain colorfast over washing.

Enzyme Mild Wash

Removes structural surface fuzz from natural cotton fibers. Secures a clean, lint-free fabric texture with ultra-soft drape properties.

Airbrush Spray Fading

Hand-applied paint splashes and visual seam spraying that give custom garments an exclusive high-streetwear industrial edge.

Premium Silicon Softening

A deep hydration bath for fibers that seals high elastic recovery and gives the cotton a silky, premium retail touch.

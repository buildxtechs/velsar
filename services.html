Manufacturing Services

What We Do Best

Our Capabilities

From raw combed yarns to finished packaging cartons, we manage all apparel manufacturing phases under single factory roofs.

Custom Manufacturing

Complete cut-make-trim (CMT) services based on custom size charts, tailored specs, and design panels. Plotting and grading handled by professional CAD tools.

Corporate Uniforms

Premium corporate uniforms, office polo shirts, work overalls, and security shirts. Engineered with breathable fabrics and double-stitched joints for maximum durability.

Active & Sportswear

Quick-dry vests, athletic t-shirts, double-layered mesh shorts, and tailored jogging pants. Advanced moisture wicking fibers prevent odors and chafing during sports.

Event T-Shirts

Rapid high-volume bulk production for marathon events, product promotion campaigns, music festivals, and institutional merchandise sets.

Special Wash Effects

High-fashion custom fabric treatments including acid washing, vintage enzyme weathering, lava dyeing finishes, tie-dye loops, and cold-pigment treatments (CPT).

Detailed Embroidery

Computerized high-density embroidery, raised 3D embroidery textures, custom badge attachments, and detailed chest logo threading using premium sheen threads.

Stone Work Detailing

High-end heat-set rhinestone placements, premium crystal layouts, and custom glass stone hot-fix detailing designed for luxury lifestyle collections.

Custom Spray Effects

Hand-crafted paint splatters, visual airbrush fades, and seam-line visual spray contrasts. Ideal for street wear collections looking for unique aesthetics.

Packaging & Labeling

Bespoke woven tags, size loops, cardboard neck hangers, printed polybags with customized warning details, and carton retail barcodes.

Bespoke Graphics Finishes

Specialty Printing Services

We carry advanced semi-automatic screen-printing carousels and professional industrial printers to achieve vibrant graphics.

DTF (Direct-to-Film)

Exceptional high-definition full-color graphic prints. Ideal for complex artworks, photographic prints, and multi-colored brand logos with seamless gradients.

DTG (Direct-to-Garment)

Uses water-based eco-friendly textile inks sprayed directly into fabric fibers. Extremely soft feel, zero-feel hand, and excellent wash durability on white/light cottons.

Puff & 3D Printing

Specialty expanding screen print ink that rises when heated, creating raised, pillowy volumetric lettering. Highly sought-after in premium streetwear hoodie lines.

Industrial Screen Printing

The industry benchmark for massive bulk orders. Exceptional economy at scale, vibrant plastisol inks, clean water-based prints, or discharge inks for breathable graphics.

Reflective & Neon

Incorporates high-visibility micro-glass beads that shine back when hit by lights. Excellent detail overlays on athletic gear, active wear, and urban safety jackets.

Patch Work & Applique

Embroidery integration with fabric layers. Custom twill, denim, or leather lettering sewn down securely to add tactile premium depth to crewnecks.

Silicon Gel Printing

Glossy raised gel overlay that offers high grip texture and a premium futuristic plastic shine. Very popular on side stripes or custom branding panels.

Discharge Screen Prints

Chemically strips out original fabric dyes and replaces them with pigment. Results in absolutely soft, zero-ink hand textures perfect for vintage worn aesthetics.

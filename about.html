Precision Fabrication & Cutting

Advanced fabric layouts are drafted using specialized computer-aided design (CAD) systems. Once locked, computerized band-knife cutting and straight-line cutting machines slice layers of fabrics with absolute zero structural margins.

This meticulous slicing prevents seam twists, aligns graphic directions, and yields a flawless drape once assembled on the sewing floors.

Advanced Stitching & Overlocking

Our sewing floors house advanced heavy-duty flatlock, overlock, and lockstitch machines (featuring premium Singer and Pegasus components). Heavyweight French Terries and delicate single jerseys are handled by highly trained seamstresses.

Special flatlock seams are deployed on athletic and heavy street wears to prevent skin chafing and secure maximum burst threshold at high pressure joints.

Industrial Production Standards

Factory Work Process & Guidelines

We adhere strictly to international manufacturing workflows, ensuring every phase—from raw yarn circular knitting to final AQL 2.5 retail dispatch—is certified for excellence.

01

Knitting

Advanced circular knitting looms convert premium combed and organic yarns into dense, high-grade single jerseys, fleeces, and rib structures.

02

Dyeing

Eco-friendly international standard dyeing vessels apply vibrant, organic color pigment setups featuring superior colorfastness scores.

03

Fabric Roll

Dyed fabrics are stabilized, relaxed, and wrapped in secure rolls to allow organic structures to recover elastic qualities prior to processing.

04

GSM Check

Rigorous fabric weight and thickness auditing. Electronic weight scales verify the exact GSM matches our clients fabric specifications.

05

Cutting Machine 1 & 2

CAD-guided precision slicing systems. Computerized heavy duty band knife and straight cutting machines 1 & 2 handle layered panels without pucker.

06

Stitching

High-speed professional Pegasus overlock and flatlock floors secure structural joints, double-collar reinforce tapes, and smooth edge trims.

07

Checking

100% manual panel audit. Master checkers verify stitching integrity, detect needle marks, and enforce absolute symmetry guidelines.

08

Ironing & Packing

Premium vacuum steam ironing eliminates creases. Garments are individually tagged, boxed, and packed in custom protective polybags.

09

Finishing Goods

Final export quality audit (AQL 2.5) check. The clean packed batches are logged into dispatch inventory for direct global retail distribution.
